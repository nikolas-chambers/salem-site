Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

The Salem app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app data is loaded from local resources and public APIs. No user accounts, analytics, or tracking are implemented.

Location Data

The app may request location permission solely to show your position on the map. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never sent to any server. Location permission can be denied or revoked at any time without affecting other app features.

Network Requests

The app makes the following network requests:

  • National Weather Service API — for weather forecasts and current conditions
  • OpenStreetMap tile servers — for map rendering
  • Google Gemini API — for the AI chat feature (only when you send a message)

No identifying information is included in these requests beyond what is necessary for the API to function (e.g., GPS coordinates for weather lookups).

Third-Party Services

Links within the app open external websites in your device browser. Those sites operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
